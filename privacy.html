Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2025

Overview

TinyRests is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our nap schedule calculator.

Information We Don't Collect

TinyRests is a simple calculator tool that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculations happen directly in your browser.

Local Storage

We use browser local storage only to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode). This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

TinyRests does not use cookies to track you or collect any information about your usage patterns.

Shareable URLs

When you use the "Share" or "Copy Link" feature, the URL contains parameters that represent your input values (such as age, wake-up time, and bedtime). These parameters are only used to restore your calculator settings if you or someone you share the link with visits that URL.

Third-Party Services

TinyRests may load common third-party libraries like Bootstrap for styling. These libraries are loaded directly from their CDNs and their usage is subject to their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us
